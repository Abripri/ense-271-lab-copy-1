## Background and Goals

### Background
  * A wireframe: A blueprint of a website, as well as a way to visualize and plan out a design. It shows the proposed layout, structure, and placement of webpage compenents.
  * Low Fidelity (lo-fi) Wireframe: A basic blueprint that is done to simply show the structure of a webpage. They are rough, not in colour, and don't show a lot of detail.
  * High Fidelity (Hi-fi) Wireframe: A detailed blueprint that shows colours, images, font styles, and more, in addition to structure and layout.

### Goals
  * To find an already completed website design on [Dibbble](https://dribbble.com)
  * To recreate sections of this design on [Figma](https://www.figma.com)
